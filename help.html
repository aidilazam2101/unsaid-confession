Everything you need to know about UNSAID

üß† What is UNSAID?

UNSAID is a safe, anonymous space designed for you to express thoughts, feelings, and confessions that are hard to say out loud. Whether it's love, sadness, regret, or joy‚Äîevery word you share here is a step toward healing. Your confessions help you release emotions, build your journey through achievements, and earn XP as you grow in the UNSAID universe.

‚úçÔ∏è How to submit a confession?

It's simple! Click on the Submit page in the navbar above. Write your confession (up to 1000 characters), choose a category that fits your emotion (Love, Sadness, Regret, etc.), and hit the submit button. Your confession will be reviewed and then appear in the community feed for others to read anonymously. Every submission earns you 10 XP!

üèÜ Achievements & XP System

Every confession you submit earns you 10 XP. As you accumulate XP, you'll unlock ranks and achievements! Start as a "First Whisper" and work your way up to "Neon Legend" by sharing 100+ confessions. Check your Profile and Achievement pages to track your progress, see which badges you've unlocked, and discover what's next on your journey.

üìå Frequently Asked Questions

Is my confession truly anonymous?

Yes! Your username is never shown to other users in the feed. While admins can see submissions for moderation purposes, your identity remains private to the community.

Can I edit or delete my confession?

Currently, editing or deleting confessions is not available. Once submitted, confessions are permanent. This feature may be added in future updates based on user feedback.

Why can't I see my achievements unlocking?

Achievements unlock automatically when you reach the required number of confessions. Make sure to check the Achievement page to see your progress bars. Some achievements may take time to appear‚Äîrefresh the page if needed!

How long does it take for my confession to appear?

Confessions are reviewed by admins before appearing in the feed. This typically happens within 24 hours. Check back soon to see your confession live!

What categories can I choose from?

You can choose from: Love & Heartbreak, Sadness & Grief, Regret & Mistakes, Anger & Frustration, Joy & Happiness, Fear & Anxiety, Hope & Dreams, and Secret Confession.
